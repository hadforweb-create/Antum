## Design Direction
- Product name: ANTUM
- Clean, modern, professional
- iOS-style glassy UI (iOS 26-inspired)
- Minimal UI, fast interactions
- Dark-first with clean contrast
- No heavy borders
- Soft surfaces, cards, subtle dividers
- Black primary buttons
- Purple accent only (not dominant)

## Terminology
- “Reels” → **Posts** everywhere
- Simple language, no social-media jargon
